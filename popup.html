Voice Assistant & TTS Extension

About

This extension enhances web accessibility by providing voice assistant and text-to-speech functionalities. Easily navigate the web, perform search queries, and have webpage content read aloud.

User Guide

  • Activate Voice Assistant: Press Ctrl+Shift+V.
  • Navigate with Keyboard: Use Tab and arrow keys to move and hear content read aloud.
  • Voice Commands: Say "search for [query]" to perform a search.

Useful Keyboard Shortcuts

  • YouTube: Press F for full screen, K to play/pause, M to mute/unmute, Arrow Left for rewind, and Arrow Right for fast forward.
  • Web Navigation: Press Ctrl+L to focus on the address bar, Ctrl+T to open a new tab, Ctrl+W to close the current tab.
  • Search: Press Ctrl+F to open the find bar, Ctrl+K to jump to the search bar.
  • Reading and Accessibility: Press Ctrl++ to zoom in, Ctrl+- to zoom out, Ctrl+0 to reset zoom level.
  • High Contrast Mode: Press Ctrl+Shift+H to toggle high contrast mode.
  • Screen Magnification: Press Ctrl+Shift+M to toggle screen magnification.
